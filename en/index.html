FAQ

1) Which languages do you translate from? Oral translation services are offered only from Russian. Written translation services are available for Russian, Ukrainian, and German. Translations from other languages can be arranged through qualified specialists who confirm their proficiency level.


2) How quickly is the translation completed? The timeline depends on the volume and complexity of the text, typically ranging from 1 to 4 days.


3) Do you provide a certificate of translation? Yes, a certificate is provided with the translation, including the address, phone number, signature, and date.


4) Do you notarize the translation? Notarization is available upon request and for an additional fee. The notary stamp can be apostilled at the local authority.


5) In what format will the translation be provided? Usually in PDF or DOCX format, other formats are available upon request.


6) How will we receive the translation? Online or by mail (for an additional fee). If you are located in LA, an in-person meeting is possible.
